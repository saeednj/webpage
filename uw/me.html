Me




I was born and raised in Tehran, Iran but currently live in Waterloo, Canada.

My name is Saeed. It is an Arabic word, but commonly used in Iran as a first name. In Arabic it means happy/merry, but in Iran it generally means blessed. In native script it is written like: سعید and it is pronounced like [s@ i:d] in IPA ASCII phonetics. My last name is also read as [ne dZa:ti].

You can find me here: Github, Twitter, Linkedin, Instagram, and some other social networks.
